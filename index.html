Our Mission

Abbie's Angels is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit created to surround caregivers and families in our community with practical help, encouragement, and connection as they navigate complex medical needs with their differently abled children.

Through events, resources, and partnerships with local organizations, we aim to make sure no caregiver feels alone in their hardest seasons.

What We Do

Caregiver Support

Helping families find the right resources during and after a medical crisis, from parking and meal expenses to in-home support services, and equipment. This way they spend less time searching and more time caring.

Events & Gatherings

Host annual events like our Winter Gala to celebrate families, raise awareness, and sustain support programs throughout the year.

Community Outreach

Partner with local hospitals, schools, organizations, and businesses to reach caregivers who need encouragement, financial assistance, and connection to specialized services.

Events

Abbie's Angels hosts special events that bring light into difficult seasons and raise funds to sustain our mission all year.

  • Annual Winter Gala with dinner, auctions, and inspiring stories.
  • Community fundraisers and awareness nights with local partners.
  • Future events highlighting families and caregivers in our community.

Ways to Help

Every gift, share, and hour of service helps us walk alongside more families in honor of Abbie and all of our community’s warriors.

  • Make a one‑time or recurring donation to support caregiver programs and events.
  • Sponsor an event, family, or specific initiative as an individual or business.
  • Volunteer at events, help with set‑up/tear‑down, or serve on a planning committee.
  • Share our posts and invite others to follow along on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

Online giving, checks, and in‑kind donations all make a tangible difference. To arrange a donation or sponsorship, you can give through our Auctria page, mail a gift, or reach out to our team to talk about the best way to help.
